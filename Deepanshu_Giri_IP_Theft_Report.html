Intellectual Property Theft Exposed

Deepanshu Giri markets himself as a prodigy who "decoded ancient texts" to bring revolutionary astrological techniques to the masses. The forensic reality is much darker: it is a case of blatant intellectual property theft.

The BCP Plagiarism

The Stolen Technique: The highly marketed "Paribhraman Paddhati" is nothing more than a rebranded version of the Bhrigu Chakra Paddhati (BCP).

BCP was extensively researched and taught by Sunil John of Saptarishis Astrology. Deepanshu Giri was a student of Sunil John. After acquiring the course material, Giri launched Lunar Astro, rebranded the technique, and claimed it as his own original decoding of ancient scriptures.

Erasing the Guru

Archived versions of Giri's early websites show a clear disclaimer: "Dedicated to Sunil John." Once Lunar Astro gained commercial traction, this acknowledgment was quietly scrubbed from the internet to maintain the illusion of a "self-taught" master from a 7-generation lineage.
